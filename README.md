# WatchStop

**A Multi-Functional Location Services Android App**

---

## Overview

WatchStop is an Android app that combines multiple location services into one place: location-triggered alarms, real-time group location sharing, and route tracking with playback. It is designed for commuters, students, parents, and social groups.

---

## Who is WatchStop For?

| User Category | What WatchStop can do for you |
|----------|---------|
|Secondary & University students (13–25) | Never miss your bus or Metro stop again |
|Parents/Guardians of commuting students | Track your child's journey for peace of mind |
| Working adults | Reliable stop alerts for your daily commute |
| Social groups & event meetups | Easier coordination with live location sharing |
| Route planners & trackers | Record and replay your movement path |

## Features

### 1. Geofence Pool
A Geofence is a zone you define on the map. Geofences are used by GeoAlarms and Group Collaboration to detect when you or others arrive somewhere.

- **Circular**: tap to place a centre point, then adjust the radius with a slider
- **Freehand Polygon**: draw any shape directly on the map
- Geofences are saved to your account and accessible across all features

### 2. GeoAlarms
Set an alarm that goes off automatically when you enter a location: no more missing your stop.

- Link any alarm to a Geofence you have created
- Schedule alarms by date, day of the week, time window, or leave them always-on
- Toggle alarms on or off individually at any time
- When triggered: loud ringtone + vibration, getting louder until you dismiss it
- Works in the background even when your screen is off

### 3. Route Tracker
Record your journey and replay it afterwards on a map.

- Tap play to start recording; your path is drawn on the map as you move
- The line is colour-coded by speed: **blue** for slow, **red** for fast
- After recording, drag a slider to see exactly where you were at any point in time
- Tap the location pin to see your coordinates and speed at that moment
- Summary panel shows total distance, duration, and average speed
- Save routes to the cloud and reload them later

### 4. Group Collaboration
Share live locations with family, friends, or your group during an event or trip.

- Create a group, assign a destination Geofence, and invite members
- See everyone's live location on a map, updated every 3 seconds
- Set your trip status: **Inactive**, **Travelling**, or **Arrived**
- Get notified automatically when you arrive at the group's destination
- Turn location sharing on or off at any time

#### Role System

Every group has three role levels so the right people stay in control:

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Member** | View locations, share own location, update trip status, apply to become Admin |
| **Admin** | Everything a Member can do, plus invite/remove members, edit group info, approve Admin applications. Can be voted out by the group |
| **SuperAdmin** | Everything an Admin can do, plus cannot be removed by vote. Assigned only when creating a group |

- Any member can vote to demote an Admin: demotion happens automatically once more than half the group votes in favour
- SuperAdmins can demote an Admin immediately
- The **Updates** tab shows pending group invitations, Admin applications, and demotion votes

## Login & Account

- Create an account with a display name, email, and password (minimum 6 characters)
- Sign in and out from the user icon in the top bar
- Change your password or switch between light and dark mode from your profile page
- Your preferences are saved to your account

---

## Accessibility

- **Large Font Mode**: increases all text to 130% size; toggle from the menu in the top bar
- **Dark / Light Mode**: switch anytime from the top bar or your profile page
- **Onboarding Tutorial**: an introduction to all four features, accessible anytime from the menu
